Sportable

We are focused on creating the most effective application for planning, managing, scheduling, and observing the players and teams involved. The goal of this application is to be versatile in the area of sports management. The application provides users of different privileges to have different levels of access to functions on the application. These functions include but are not limited to: enrolling athletes into clubs, managing the attendance of the athletes, delegate workouts, access training calendars for workouts, send out emails to others involved, send payment reminders and make payments.
